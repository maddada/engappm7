######## COMMANDS FOR ANDROID RELEASE:
------Keystore:
To create the keystore:
keytool -genkey -v -keystore memar-release-key.keystore -alias memar -keyalg RSA -keysize 2048 -validity 10000

To use the keystore:
jarsigner -verbose -sigalg SHA1withRSA -digestalg SHA1 -keystore memar-release-key.keystore memar-release-v1_0.apk memar
